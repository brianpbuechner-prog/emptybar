📍 The Moment

Your vote in Hastings is worth fighting for

Across Minnesota and the country, access to the ballot is being quietly narrowed through purged voter rolls, reduced polling locations, and legislation designed to make registration harder. We're keeping a close eye on it.

This issue, we're focused on what's happening to voting rights, and what you can do about it right now from your Hastings zip code.

🌉 Local Spotlight

Hastings shows up

This fall, Hastings Progressives hopes to table at local community events to help neighbors check their registration status, find their polling location, and get their questions answered.

👁 What We're Watching

Three things on our radar

Federal Voting Legislation

Bills in Congress would impose new federal ID requirements and restrict same-day registration. Minnesota's same-day registration is one of the strongest in the country, and we're tracking any attempt to override state protections.

MN Legislative Session

State lawmakers have debated automatic voter registration and expanded early voting access. We'll keep you posted on what passed, what stalled, and what's coming back next session.

Election Administration Funding

Local clerks and county election offices are underfunded heading into a heavy election cycle. It's not glamorous, but it matters. We're paying attention so you can hold your local officials accountable.
